    
** 1st version of VISA drivers/controlers for Agilent E4980A (LCR meter) **
   
This 1st version includes:
   
- Initialization of Instrument-PC communication via VISA library.
- Configuration of Instrument for 2 types of DUT (Element trace, Dielectric)
  as well as two measurement setups (single frequency, frequency sweep)
- Trigers the instrument and recovers measurements to be stored in a list
  type array ("result")
